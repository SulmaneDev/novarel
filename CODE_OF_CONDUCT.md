# Code of Conduct

## Our Philosophy

Novarel is a project created and maintained with **love** and deep care by its creators and the amazing community of contributors. We believe that a great framework is built not just with code, but with kindness, respect, and a shared passion for excellence.

## Our Standards

To foster a welcoming and productive environment, we expect every participant to:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat everyone with dignity, regardless of their background or level of experience.
- **Lead with Kindness**: Remember that behind every commit and comment is a person. We are all here to learn and grow together.
- **Collaborate with Love**: Share your knowledge freely and support your fellow contributors.
- **Maintain Integrity**: Be honest, ethical, and take responsibility for your actions.

## Our Goal

Our goal is to create a community where everyone feels valued and inspired to build something great. By contributing to Novarel, you agree to uphold these values and help us maintain an atmosphere of mutual support and professional excellence.

---

Thank you for being a part of Novarel and for helping us build this project with love.
